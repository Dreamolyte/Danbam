Danbam

Danbam is a korean restaurant that serves you traditional food with high taste and guaranteed quality by a touch of modern culture. Danbam created on 2020 by Jos Raf Tur, in Jakarta, Kemanggisan. Our very first branch was a small restaurant on the side of the road, 3 people running the restaurant, and the bussiness gotten bigger each day until we owned several branches in Java island.

Danbam offers traditional, very delicious food and an amazing neon themed vibes. from the outside looks of the building, you would have the feeling of excitement of being in a cool club but instead of a club it's a korean restaurant. You would be enjoying and experiencing its cozyness, and relaxing atmosphere, filled with traditional korean music which makes it so comfortable.

We use family receipe that have been passed down from generation to generation by our predecessor, so our food tastes delicious and authentic. supported by a highly professional quality services and with the beautiful neon surroundings, wishing you an unforgetable experience.
